## About Whiteport Design Studio (WDS)

WDS is an AI-augmented design methodology created by M√•rten Angner, UX designer and founder of Whiteport, a design and development agency from Sweden. WDS is a plugin module for the BMad Method - an open-source AI-augmented development framework. 

**The Mission:** Give designers everywhere free access to AI agents specifically tailored for design work, while preserving and amplifying their creative thinking through specifications.
